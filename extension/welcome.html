Save Image As — Installed!

Convert and save any image as PNG, JPG, WebP, or AVIF

1

Right-click any image

Find any image on a webpage and right-click it to open the context menu.

2

Select "Save Image As"

Hover over "Save Image As" to see available formats.

3

Choose your format

Pick PNG, JPG, WebP, or AVIF. The image is converted instantly in your browser and downloaded.
